Radius mark.
 
  File name : RMARK1.LSP


   Example-1 Use two points
  Command: rmark1   
  2Point or <Select Arc or Circle>: 2p [Enter '2P' to choose 2 point option]
  Pick first point: {p1} [Pick start point of mark line]
  Pick second point:  mid of {p2} [Pick end point of mark line]
  Enter Text: R=300 [Enter text] 
  


 
   Example-2 Select circle or arc entity
  Command: rmark1
  2Point or <Select Arc or Circle>: mid of {p1}   
    [Just Pick a point on arc entity. program recognizes radius of arc, and draws radius mark]
