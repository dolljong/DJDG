Align texts to vertical line.
 
  Program name : TAV.LSP


  Civil engineering drawings seem to have a lot of tables. When you write all the numbers in a table, 
sometimes the numbers are arranged in a crooked manner. Isn't it easy to align them? This program 
has a function to arrange these numbers in a line by left, center or right.
